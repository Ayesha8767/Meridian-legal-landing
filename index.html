Art. III — Who We Are

A brief built on access, not just advocacy.

Meridian exists on the belief that access to justice is an infrastructure problem as much as a legal one. We connect independent lawyers with the tools, training and workspace they need to serve clients that traditional firms overlook.

What started as a reading group of six practitioners in 2016 is now a working collective of over four hundred lawyers, researchers and legal-technology founders across the continent.
